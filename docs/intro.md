# Introduction

Welcome — these are my personal notes on philosophy: a growing collection of summaries, reflections, and ideas gathered from studying the great thinkers.

## What's here

The notes are organized by philosopher. Each page covers a thinker's life, core ideas, key works, and what I find worth carrying forward.

Start anywhere that interests you. The  re's no required reading order.

## Journal

Alongside the notes, I keep a [Journal](/journal). The Stoics — Marcus Aurelius, Epictetus, Seneca — wrote to themselves as a daily practice, not for an audience. This journal follows the same spirit: honest reflection over polished prose.

> *"The unexamined life is not worth living."* — Socrates
